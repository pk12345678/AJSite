Abhijith Jayakumar

Researcher working on Quantum Computing and Machine Learning.

I am a PhD student working with Prof Apoorva Patel at the Centre for High Energy Physics, Indian Institute of Science. The main focus of my research are quantum walk based algorithms.

In 2020, I interned at Los Alamos National Lab, working on developing efficient algorithms for learning graphical models.

I have an MTech in Computational Science from Department of Computational and Data Sciences, IISc. And before that I did my undergraduate studies at NIT Trichy
